Learn More About The School

From 2010, a group of friends who worshipped with the Seventh-day Adventist church in the United State of America met and discussed best possible ways to help their nation Ghana. They said “ we have to give back to society after so many years in the Diaspora”. They planned to put together some sort of legacy for Ghanaian back home. Pastor Dr. IB Boateng, a leadership coach, lecturer, minister of the gospel was the initiator of this whole idea.
